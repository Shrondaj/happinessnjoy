Our Services

Grief Support

Individual and group counseling to help you navigate loss and find meaning in your healing journey. We provide a safe space to process emotions and honor your loved ones.

  • Individual grief counseling
  • Support groups
  • Family grief therapy
  • Memorial services guidance

Trauma Therapy

Evidence-based trauma treatment approaches including EMDR, CBT, and somatic therapies to help you reclaim your life and build resilience.

  • PTSD treatment
  • EMDR therapy
  • Somatic experiencing
  • Trauma-informed care

Addiction Recovery

Holistic addiction treatment that addresses the whole person. We support sustainable recovery through community, therapy, and spiritual wellness.

  • Individual addiction counseling
  • Family therapy
  • Recovery support groups
  • Relapse prevention

Our Holistic Approach

At Happiness & Joy, we believe that true healing encompasses the whole person - mind, body, and spirit. Our community-centered approach creates a supportive environment where individuals and families can find hope and healing.

Mind

Evidence-based therapeutic approaches that address mental health and emotional wellbeing

Body

Somatic therapies and wellness practices that honor the body's role in healing

Spirit

Spiritual support and meaning-making that honors your personal beliefs and values
